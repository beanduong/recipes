# Cookies

## Ingredients

- 130g unsalted, brown butter
- 30g cold water
- 55g sugar
- 125g dark brown sugar
- 5g vanilla syrup
- 3g kosher salt
- 1 large egg, fridge cold
- 5g baking powder
- 175g flour

## Add-ins
- 200g semi-sweet chocolate chips

### White Chocolate Dark Cookie
- 200g white chocolate
- 40g cocoa powder
- subtract 40g flour
- add 10g coldwater

### Cranberry White Choc
- 50g cranberries
- 100g white chocolate chips

### Blueberry White Choc
- 125g blueberries
- 100g white chocolate chips
- 10g blueberry flavour

## Comments
- 100g regular/brown sugar had good texture, but felt like too much sugar
- 70g sugar and 70g dark brown sugar got too cakey
- will try 90g regular/brown sugar next time
