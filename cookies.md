# Cookies

## Ingredients

- 150g unsalted butter
- 30g cold water
- 70g sugar
- 70g dark brown sugar
- 1 large egg, fridge cold
- 15ml vanilla extract
- 5g baking powder
- 3g kosher salt
- 175g all-purpose flour
- 150g semi-sweet chocolate chips
- Flaky sea salt, for finishing


- 100g bittersweet chocolate
- 25g cocoa powder
